Array Studio was built for organizations doing visible work in the real world: public art teams, civic initiatives, destinations, developers, cultural institutions, and brands with a public mission.

We are not a narrow production vendor. We step in earlier, help define the narrative, build the creative system, and make sure the message keeps working once the program leaves the deck and enters the street.

Our best work happens when strategy, design, content, and rollout are treated as one system. That is how public programs feel coherent, credible, and worth showing up for.
